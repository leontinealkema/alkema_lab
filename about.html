Overview

We are passionate about collaborating with substantive experts to improve estimation methods. To that end, we collaborate with various United Nations groups, the WHO, and other organizations in the areas of global health and family planning.

Projects

Area Group/initiative Main methodological contributions
Family planning UNPD, FP2020 The family planning estimation tool (FPET) is used to provide estimates and projections of contraceptive use and unmet need for contraceptives by the UNPD since 2013 and for annual reporting for the FP2020 intiative.
Child mortality UN IGME, led by UNICEF The Bayesian B-spline bias-adjustment method (B3) for estimating the under-five mortality rate for all countries; Bayesian sparse regression model with temporal smoothing for estimating stillbirth rates.
Maternal mortality UN MMEIG, led by WHO The Bayesian maternal mortality model (BMat) for estimating the maternal mortality ratio; Bayesian bivariate random walk model (BMIs) for estimating sensitivity and specificity in reporting of maternal mortality in vital registration data.

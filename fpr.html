FPR

  • FPR

FPR, the family planning research group, aims to contribute to improved information on family planning (FP) indicators and related outcomes in populations worldwide. The group focuses on

  • Compilation and assessment of FP data sources, including service statistics data
  • Development of statistical models for monitoring and forecasting of FP indicators and evaluation of FP initiatives
  • Analyses of levels and trends in FP indicators, their relation with associated outcomes, and the impact of FP interventions
  • Working with local stakeholders to make available data, tools and research findings for use in program planning
